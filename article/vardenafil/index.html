Vardenafil

Medication that chemically assists erections of the penis

Vardenafil may also be known as Levitra.

What does it do?

  • Lower body

    Reduction of erectile dysfunction

Vardenafil takes around half an hour to take effect, and the effects may last for several hours.

More information

Alternatives to vardenafil include tadalafil and sildenafil.

You can read more about erectile dysfunction treatments on the NHS Choices website.
