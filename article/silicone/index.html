Silicone

Subcutaneous injection of 'filler' material to create the appearance of body fat, e.g. on cheeks, hips or buttocks, but which is extremely dangerous

Silicone may also be known as Filler.

What does it do?

  • Lower body

    Bigger hips or buttocks

The effects are permanent but frequently involve very serious medical complications.

More information

⚠ Warning

Silicone filler injections are extrememly dangerous and can result in permanent disfigurement (Hage, Kanhai, Oen, van Diest, & Karim, 2001) or death (Clark, Cantrell, Pacal, Chen, & Betten, 2008). Do not attempt this.

Silicone injections are different from modern breast augmentation, which is significantly safer.

  2. Clark, R. F., Cantrell, F. L., Pacal, A., Chen, W., & Betten, D. P. (2008). Subcutaneous silicone injection leading to multi-system organ failure. Clinical Toxicology, 46(9), 834–837.
