Thyroid chondroplasty

Surgical reduction of the size of the laryngeal prominence (Adam's apple)

Thyroid chondroplasty may also be known as Tracheal shave or Chondrolaryngoplasty.

What does it do?

  • Upper body

    Reduced Adam's apple

This treatment is permanent.

More information
